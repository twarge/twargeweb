Privacy Policy

Summary

Twarge does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Twarge apps have no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising, no crash reporting, no user accounts, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage data, or any other information about you. Apple may collect this data as part of the operating system crash reporting, and you are free to turn that off.

Your documents

Your documents stay on your device, or in locations you choose (such as iCloud Drive or other storage you configure). Twarge never uploads, copies, or transmits your documents or their contents to us or to anyone else.

Network access

The app may connect to the internet to a URL of the user's choosing. Typeset can pull sources and packages from the Typst Universe. We do not have access to those connections, nor do we have a way to collect that data. Twarge does not have access to those logs.

Third parties

We do not share data with third parties, because we have no data to share.

Data retention and deletion

We retain no data about you, so there is nothing for us to delete.

Children's privacy

Twarge apps do not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Since the app collects nothing, we expect changes to be rare.
